## Special Rules
### Mutations
Heroes can buy up to 2 random mutations. The first costs 30 gold and the second costs 40 gold. These mutations don't count towards their skill limit.
### Mutation: Wing
Can make Agi check before Engaging or Moving to gain the flight properties. Failed flight roll counts as a failed charge if the flight was required to reach the target. Flight Agi checks are made with advantage.
### Mutation: Bile-Puker
Gains Bile Spew: Range 10" covering a 2" diameter area effect from point. Roll attack per unit in the area of effect. Usable once per 2 turns. Can hit allies and units in melee. If a unit is hit that is in melee, all connected units are also hit.
### Mutation: Claw
Gain one extra attack that must be the Claw, treat as a Sword. Gains Berserker.
### Mutation: Extra Arm
Gain one Melee equipment slot. This lets you wield an extra weapon or Shield as though you had 3 melee Equipment slots. Cannot be taken if this unit has the "No Equipment" skill.
### Mutation: Tentacle
Gain one extra attack that must be the Tentacle. When attacking with a tentacle the attack always Stuns regardless of the Injury Roll. Enemies have disadvantage against the tentacle.
### Mutation: Exploding Husk
Explodes when Incapacitated, all units within 3" diameter take Melee 12 attack.
### Mutation: Leechspawn
Gain one extra wound. If this unit deals a wound to an enemy they can recover one wound up to their maximum wound threshold.

## Skills 
### Leader
*Inherent*

Allied, non-animal units within 5" of this unit can use this unit's Morale stat for Checks instead of their own.
### Cult Magic
*Spellcasting*

This unit can cast Cult Magic.
### Apprentice Spellcaster
*Inherent*

This unit makes all spellcasting checks with a -2 to the roll.
### Free mutation
*Inherent*

This unit gets one mutation of choice for free.
### No Equipment
*Inherent*

This unit may not have any equipment, but does not suffer the penalties for fighting Unarmed.
### Fear
*Morale*

This unit causes Fear.
### Berserker
*Inherent*

At the end of the Engage Phase, if there is an enemy within Line-of-Sight and Engage range, this unit attempts to Engage the closest such unit.
