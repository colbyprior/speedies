| Max Units | 15 |

## Special Rules
### Mercenary Background
At warband creation pick one Mercenary Background skill to modify your warband.
### Mercenary Background: Marauders
All heroes get +2 to melee and +1 to injury.
### Mercenary Background: Rangers
All Marksman get +2 to ranged.
### Mercenary Background: Merchants
Start with 100 extra gold. Halve any future upkeep costs.

## Skills 
### Leader
*Inherent*

Allied units with 6" of this unit can use this morale stat for checks instead of their own.
### Fear
*Inherent*

This unit causes Fear.
### Large
*Inherent*

This units size makes them easier to hit with ranged attacks. They suffer a -3 defense penalty against ranged.
### Big Hitter 1
*Inherent*

This unit gets +2 Inj and +2 Piercing for melee attacks.
### Upkeep 1
*Inherent*

You must pay this unit 10g at the end of each battle. If you are unable to pay this gold the unit will not be available for your next battle.
### Bull Rush
*Melee*

This unit gets advantage on the first round of combat when they engage an enemy
